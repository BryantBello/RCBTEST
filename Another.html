How to make a Sandwich

  1. Pick out your bread of choice
  2. Slice the bread into two pieces
  3. Pick out your lunchmeat
  4. Pick out your cheese
  5. Pick out condiments of choice
  6. Place the lunchmeat on the bottom half of chosen bread
  7. Place the cheese on top of the lunchmeat
  8. Put condiments on top of cheese
  9. Put other half of bread on top of condiments
  10. Cut the sandwich in half
  11. Eat the sandwich and enjoy
